Ear Association

Welcome to Ear Association! This is a place to exchange music recommendations by drawing associations between previous suggestions and new pieces. The most recently shared song was Me the Machine by Imogen Heap.

This project was inspired by the evenings Jacob and Mohan would spend sitting around Mohan's stereo, eating pizza, talking about life, and swapping music recommendations.

Ear Association is run by Evy Kassirer and Jacob deGroot-Maggetti, but we welcome contributions by others! If one of the pieces on the site suggests an association to you, let either Jacob or Evy know!
