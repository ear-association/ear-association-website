The Pleasure Will Be Mine - David Curley and Mick Broderick

I love the finger picking, and the steady movement of chords in the guitar part in Drifting reminded me of this beautiful song I heard at the Goderich Celtic College a couple of summers ago. I imagine Drifting would make good cooking music, where The Pleasure Will Be Mine is much slower, perhaps for when you're finishing eating dinner. Both of them, though, hold a kind of acoustic beauty that makes me love the guitar and, in the case of The Pleasure Will Be Mine, the violin and voice.

The Pleasure Will Be Mine was suggested by Caleb deGroot-Maggetti in response to Andy McKee's Drifting.

It reminded Brian of Simon & Garfunkel's The Dangling Conversation.

It reminded Jacob of Kaki King's Teek.
